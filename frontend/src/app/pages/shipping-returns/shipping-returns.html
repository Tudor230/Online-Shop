Shipping and returns

We keep this simple: clear delivery timelines, transparent costs, and a return flow that does not waste your time. These rules are tailored for customizable electronics, where build quality and safe packaging matter.

🚚 Dispatch speed

In-stock products ship in up to 48 hours. Custom builds ship in 2-5 business days.

↩️ Return window

30 days from delivery for eligible items in good condition.

💳 Refund speed

Refunds are issued within 3-5 business days after inspection approval.

📍 Tracking and delivery issues

  • Tracking numbers are sent by email immediately after dispatch.
  • If tracking has no movement for 48 hours, contact us and we open a carrier claim right away.
  • Visible transit damage should be photographed before opening for faster replacement handling.
  • For lost parcels, we ship a replacement or issue a full refund once the carrier confirms loss.

⏱️ Refund and exchange timing

  • Exchanges are prepared within 1-2 business days after the returned item passes inspection.
  • Refunds go back to the original payment method; bank posting can add up to 5 extra business days.
  • If a promo discount was used, your refund reflects the paid amount for the returned item.
  • Shipping fees are refunded only when the return is due to our mistake or confirmed shipping damage.
