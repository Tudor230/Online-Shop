  • Remote troubleshooting: same day to 1 business day
  • Bench diagnostics after arrival: 1-2 business days

  • Replacement dispatch: 1-2 business days once approved

📄 Proof and eligibility

  • Order number or invoice is required for any warranty claim.
  • Serial number labels must remain readable and unaltered.
  • Transferred gifts are covered if original proof of purchase is provided.
  • Business buyers get the same base hardware coverage unless contract terms state otherwise.

🧑‍🔧 After-warranty support

  • Paid diagnostics and repair are available for out-of-warranty devices.
  • We can provide upgrade recommendations if a direct repair is not cost-effective.
  • For discontinued components, we offer compatible alternatives where possible.
